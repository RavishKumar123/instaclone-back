Brown <a href="https://go.politicoemail.com/?qs=55926f57d09dbbf253cf49ee89db9a6cc935e92fb07a5da9bca20194f6ecef2b2d0dd096d5be115f5830566e475615438fceda9c48be53ee" target="_blank"><u>jams on stage with Cheap Trick</u></a>, his daughter Ayla, Warrant, and so forth, living the rock-star dream.</p>
